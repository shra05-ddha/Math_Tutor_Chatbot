tan²(2x-3)

Solution:

∫tan²(2x-3)dx = ∫[sec²(2x-3)-1]dx = I

put 2x-3 = t

so that 2dx = dt

I = \(\frac { 1 }{ 2 }\) ∫sec²t dt-x+c

= \(\frac { 1 }{ 2 }t-x+c\)

= \(\frac { 1 }{ 2 }tan(2x-3)-x+c\)
